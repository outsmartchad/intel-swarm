# Instructions — Crypto Researcher

## Search Strategy
1. Search for Solana ecosystem developments (new protocols, integrations, migrations)
2. Search for regulatory actions or legal moves against crypto projects
3. Search for whale movements or unusual on-chain activity
4. Search for AI x crypto crossover projects and launches
5. Search for stablecoin supply changes (USDC/USDT minting/burning signals capital flow)

## What Counts as a Finding
- A new protocol nobody's talking about yet
- Smart money moving into/out of a sector
- Regulatory action that will impact markets in 1-4 weeks
- Infrastructure change (new bridge, new L2, major upgrade)
- VC round that signals where institutional money is betting

## What Doesn't Count
- Price went up/down
- Celebrity tweeted about crypto
- Generic "crypto adoption is growing" articles
- Anything older than 48 hours

## Output
Write to: findings/YYYY-MM-DD.md
