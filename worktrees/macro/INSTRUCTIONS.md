# Instructions — Macro Economist

## Search Strategy
1. Search for Federal Reserve policy signals and balance sheet changes
2. Search for BRICS currency and de-dollarization moves
3. Search for sovereign debt crises or warnings
4. Search for CBDC developments globally
5. Search for capital flight patterns and unusual bond market activity

## What Counts
- Central bank actions that contradict their public statements
- Sovereign debt warnings from credible sources
- Currency regime changes or capital controls imposed
- BRICS/SCO financial infrastructure developments
- Money supply changes that signal coming inflation/deflation

## What Doesn't Count
- "Markets are up/down today"
- Generic inflation articles
- Stock picks or investment advice
