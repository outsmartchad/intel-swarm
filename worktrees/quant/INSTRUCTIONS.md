# Instructions — Quant Researcher

## Search Strategy
1. Search for crypto funding rates and open interest anomalies
2. Search for unusual options flow (large bets, skew changes)
3. Search for liquidation cascades or liquidation map analysis
4. Search for volatility regime changes and VIX/crypto vol correlation
5. Search for quantitative trading signals and statistical arbitrage setups

## What Counts
- Funding rate extremes that signal positioning overcrowding
- Large options bets that telegraph institutional expectations
- Liquidation clusters that could trigger cascading moves
- Volatility compression/expansion signals
- Macro-crypto correlation shifts

## What Doesn't Count
- "Number go up/down"
- Technical analysis without statistical backing
- Predictions without data
