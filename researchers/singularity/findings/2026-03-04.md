Over 100 GW of new DC capacity is in active planning — requiring dedicated power sources that don't yet exist. Air cooling is dead for AI; immersion cooling is now required for Blackwell-class deployments. [GPUnex](https://www.gpunex.com/blog/ai-data-center-energy-crisis/)

This is the first time implant surgery could scale beyond a handful of specialized hospitals.
