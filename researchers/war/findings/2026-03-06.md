Nakhchivan is a strategic energy and transport corridor — this is the first strike on a post-Soviet state in this conflict and opens a northern Caucasus flank. March 15 deadline for 3 US arms packages means Taiwan's legislative gridlock and Trump's Beijing channel are now directly linked.
