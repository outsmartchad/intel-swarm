## Edge Signal
China's May 1 zero-tariff announcement for all 53 African nations is being read as a trade story — but the real play is payment rails: as African-Chinese trade volumes surge, the settlement infrastructure (who processes payments, in what currency) becomes the strategic prize, and stablecoin issuers + African mobile money operators are better positioned than Western correspondent banks to capture it.

## Connects To
Pine Labs entering ME/Africa/SEA stablecoin corridors connects directly to the Nigeria Institutional Build thread (India providing the cross-border rails Nigeria's domestic regulation is building toward) and to the Ethiopia stablecoin surge thread (supply-side infrastructure meeting demand-side currency collapse adoption). China's Africa zero-tariff decision accelerates the ASEAN De-Dollarization thread — trade volumes require settlement alternatives.
