The implication: capital and product development will disproportionately flow to markets like Philippines, Vietnam, Indonesia in the next 24 months. https://www.blockhead.co/2026/03/04/southeast-asia-leads-expansion-as-apac-fintech-prioritizes-ai-inclusion-and-fraud-resilience/

## Edge Signal
The real story in ASEAN's pre-BRICS currency acceptance isn't trade — it's what happens to USD stablecoin liquidity in Southeast Asia when BRICS settles in a competing currency, and nobody in the stablecoin ecosystem is modeling this risk yet.

## Connects To
BRICS currency adoption by ASEAN directly pressures India's CBDC bridge proposal (active thread) to become the architecture that survives — India sits at the intersection of BRICS credibility and Western payment legitimacy, which is why the India CBDC bridge is more important than previously framed.
