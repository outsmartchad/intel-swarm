## Edge Signal
The convergence of on-device agent control (AGI Inc/Snapdragon, NullClaw on microcontrollers) means agents are about to bypass app stores, app UX, and app economics entirely — this is an existential threat to the App Store duopoly that nobody in the "AI replacing jobs" conversation is talking about yet.

## Connects To
OKX's OnchainOS is the onchain mirror of the same pattern: agents that bypass traditional gatekeepers (app stores on mobile, brokers/exchanges in finance) — autonomous onchain agents will route around CEX infrastructure the same way on-device agents route around app UX.
