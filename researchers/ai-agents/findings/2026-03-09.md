## Top Findings

- **Block workers blow up Dorsey's AI replacement narrative — "You can't really AI that"** — The Guardian interviewed 7 current and ex-Block employees who say AI cannot do their jobs at the scale Dorsey claims. Workers allege the 4,000-person cut (~50% of workforce) was investor optics after Block's stock slid post-crypto losses — not genuine AI productivity gains. "This was posturing for the market," one still-employed worker told Guardian. This is the first organized on-the-record pushback from inside a company claiming AI-driven mass layoffs. Source: https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2026/mar/08/block-ai-layoffs-jack-dorsey

Salesforce stock is down ~20% this year on "SaaSpocalypse" fears — that AI agents will erode its per-seat SaaS model. The company selling enterprise AI agents cut the people building enterprise AI agents. Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Salesforce / https://www.benzinga.com/markets/tech/26/03/51115886/marc-benioff-said-mass-ai-layoffs-werent-coming-then-came-a-brutal-week-for-white-collar-jobs

- **Polygon Agent CLI ships — autonomous AI agents can now manage wallets, send payments, and establish on-chain identity without human oversight** — Polygon released a CLI toolkit that gives AI agents native blockchain capabilities: wallet management, payments, digital identity, and on-chain transactions with no human in the loop. Compatible with Claude AI and other frameworks. Includes "dry run" pre-execution audit feature. This is the first production-grade on-chain infrastructure explicitly designed for autonomous AI agents, not humans using wallets. Source: https://www.cointrust.com/market-news/polygon-launches-agent-cli-for-autonomous-ai-on-blockchain

- **Circle and Stripe racing to build stablecoin payment rails for autonomous AI agents — Bloomberg reports "a world that doesn't exist yet"** — Bloomberg confirmed Circle and Stripe are both building payment infrastructure for autonomous AI agent transactions at scale, settling in stablecoins. Bloomberg's framing is telling: these companies are betting on a future where agents transact millions of times daily — but that future "barely exists" yet. The financial infrastructure layer is being built before the agents it's designed to serve are actually deployed. Source: https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-03-07/stablecoin-firms-bet-big-on-ai-agent-payments-that-barely-exist

## Edge Signal

The "AI washing" pattern is accelerating: companies are attributing layoffs to AI productivity for investor optics while the actual AI capabilities don't exist at the claimed scale — and the companies building AI products are themselves laying off the humans who build them. The gap between the CEO narrative and operational reality is widening fast, and nobody is measuring it systematically.
