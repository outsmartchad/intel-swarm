Source: https://www.argusmedia.com/en/news-and-insights/latest-market-news/2690734-iea-warns-of-lithium-and-copper-deficits-by-2035

Companies redesigning global networks, building inventory buffers against "structural volatility." Source: https://www.supplychaindive.com/news/scarcity-redefines-the-2026-supply-chain-playbook/810052/

- **Central Bank Gold Signal**: Global central banks net buyers of gold for over a decade—"structural expression of waning confidence in fiat currency systems." Silver demand building from electrification/solar infrastructure. Source: https://goldsilver.com/industry-news/article/gold-silver-prices-short-term-noise-long-term-signal/
