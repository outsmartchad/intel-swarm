# Regulatory Arbitrage Researcher - 2026-03-04

## Top Findings
- **Virginia's LLD Act (HB293) passes 59-37** — On Feb 13, 2026, Virginia became the first US state to pass a DAO LLC law, creating "Limited Liability Decentralized Autonomous Organizations." DAOs can now incorporate as legal entities with liability shields — the entityless model is officially dead. [https://blockeden.xyz/forum/t/virginia-passes-the-first-us-dao-llc-law-59-37-daos-can-now-incorporate-as-limited-liability-entities/971](https://blockeden.xyz/forum/t/virginia-passes-the-first-us-dao-llc-law-59-37-daos-can-now-incorporate-as-limited-liability-entities/971)
- **CARF went live in 50+ countries Jan 2026** — The Crypto Asset Reporting Framework activated in over 50 jurisdictions at the start of 2026, compelling foreign exchanges to share user data with tax authorities globally. The era of offshore opacity is functionally over. [https://www.coindesk.com/business/2026/02/01/the-era-of-suitcase-money-is-over-why-your-offshore-crypto-is-no-longer-safe-from-the-taxman](https://www.coindesk.com/business/2026/02/01/the-era-of-suitcase-money-is-over-why-your-offshore-crypto-is-no-longer-safe-from-the-taxman)
- **Stablecoin yield loophole under siege** — Under the GENIUS Act, stablecoin issuers can offer yield that banks legally cannot, creating a live regulatory arbitrage window. Banks are now lobbying hard to close it via RFIA amendments — this window is closing fast. [https://clsbluesky.law.columbia.edu/2026/01/23/closing-the-stablecoin-yield-loophole-in-the-post-genius-era/](https://clsbluesky.law.columbia.edu/2026/01/23/closing-the-stablecoin-yield-loophole-in-the-post-genius-era/)
- **Lido ruling kills "entityless" DAOs** — A court ruling involving Lido dismantled the concept of DAOs operating as legal voids; unstructured DAOs now risk being treated as general partnerships, exposing members to unlimited liability. Wrapper structures (foundations, UNAs) are now essential. [https://aurum.law/newsroom/DAO-3-0-ultimate-dao-legal-structuring-in-2025-and-beyond](https://aurum.law/newsroom/DAO-3-0-ultimate-dao-legal-structuring-in-2025-and-beyond)
- **Bosnia & Herzegovina emerging as stealth crypto haven** — While everyone watches Dubai and Singapore, Bosnia is quietly becoming one of Europe's most crypto-friendly jurisdictions with minimal regulatory overhead — and almost no one is talking about it. [https://gofaizen-sherle.com/blog/top-crypto-friendly-jurisdictions](https://gofaizen-sherle.com/blog/top-crypto-friendly-jurisdictions)

## Edge Signal
The stablecoin yield loophole under GENIUS Act is the most time-limited regulatory arbitrage in crypto right now — stablecoin issuers can legally offer yield that banks cannot, but banking lobbyists are already drafting the kill switch, meaning first-movers have a narrow window before RFIA closes it.

## Connects To
Virginia's LLD Act directly enables on-chain AI agent DAOs to incorporate with liability shields, which is the missing legal primitive for autonomous agent treasuries managing real capital — connects directly to the AI agents and DeFi infrastructure domains.
