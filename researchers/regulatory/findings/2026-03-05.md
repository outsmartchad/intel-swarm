Industry is scrambling; comment period closes ~April 27. ## Edge Signal
The OCC's GENIUS Act implementation is doing through regulatory interpretation what banking lobbyists couldn't achieve through legislation — it's closing the stablecoin yield loophole by redefining third-party reward programs as issuer evasion, and the industry's only remedy is a 60-day comment window that closes April 27.

## Connects To
