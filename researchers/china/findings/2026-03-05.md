# China Researcher - March 5, 2026

## Top Findings

- **Two Sessions Opens Amid Unprecedented Military Purge** — 12+ generals removed in the week before China's annual legislative gathering, including Xi's top general Zhang Youxia under corruption investigation; CSIS tallies 100+ senior officers purged since 2022 — a "staggering" rate that signals Xi is prioritizing loyalty over battlefield competence ahead of the 2027 Taiwan window. https://www.theguardian.com/world/2026/mar/03/china-two-sessions-president-xi-economy-defence-technology

- **PLA Quietly Launching New Type 09V Nuclear-Armed Submarine** — Satellite imagery (Feb 9) shows China's first Type 09V SSGN moved to launch bay at Huludao shipyard; the sub is larger than predecessors with 18 VLS cells for anti-ship/land-attack cruise missiles and an advanced X-stern rudder — specifically designed to neutralize US carrier strike groups in a Taiwan contingency. https://understandingwar.org/research/china-taiwan/china-taiwan-update-march-1-2026/

- **Taiwan Faces March 15 Deadline on US Arms Packages** — Taiwan risks losing three major US weapons deals due to domestic legislative gridlock over the special defense budget; failure to sign would mark the first time Taiwan loses weapons access due to internal politics, handing Beijing a soft victory without firing a shot. https://understandingwar.org/research/china-taiwan/china-taiwan-update-march-1-2026/

- **China's AI Tech Shock "Just Getting Started" — Huawei Clusters Closing the Gap** — TS Lombard's chief China economist warns the US "perceived monopoly" on AI is broken: China is pairing frontier-level models with emerging-market production costs, backed by an $8.69B national AI fund, massive Huawei chip clusters, and cheap energy — while China itself blocked NVIDIA H200 imports even after the US reversed Biden-era bans. https://www.cnbc.com/2026/02/16/chinas-tech-shock-ai-monopoly-us.html

- **Digital Yuan Goes Interest-Bearing, Enters New Phase of CBDC Push** — As of January 1, 2026, China's e-CNY now operates as "digital deposit currency" — commercial banks can pay interest on verified wallets, fundamentally repositioning the digital yuan to compete with traditional savings accounts and accelerate adoption as a cross-border settlement rail. https://www.forbes.com/sites/digital-assets/2026/01/22/chinas-digital-rmb-trillion-dollar-shift-in-payment-and-crypto-rails/

- **Property Slump Enters Year 5 With No Floor in Sight** — 80 million unsold/vacant homes clog the market, China Vanke (state-backed) teetering on default, and estimates suggest 80% of developers could eventually exit the industry; Beijing has quietly stopped trying to prop up housing prices and is "shrugging its shoulders and moving on." https://www.atlanticcouncil.org/blogs/econographics/chinas-property-slump-deepens-and-threatens-more-than-the-housing-sector/

## Edge Signal

The military purge happening *during* Two Sessions is the tell — Xi is institutionalizing fear at the top of the PLA right before the 2027 Taiwan capability deadline, suggesting the corruption problem is so systemic that he'd rather field a loyal-but-degraded force than a capable-but-corrupt one.

## Connects To

China's dual play — interest-bearing e-CNY for domestic adoption + Huawei AI clusters for tech sovereignty — is the same decoupling playbook applied to money and compute simultaneously, directly threatening USD settlement rails and US AI infrastructure dominance in the Global South.
