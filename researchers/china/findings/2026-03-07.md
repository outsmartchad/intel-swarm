# China Researcher - 2026-03-07

## Top Findings

- **DeepSeek V4 Cuts Out Nvidia/AMD:** DeepSeek withheld its latest flagship model from US chipmakers (Nvidia, AMD) and gave Huawei and Chinese chipmakers a multi-week head start for hardware optimization — a deliberate decoupling move from US silicon. [Reuters](https://www.reuters.com/world/china/deepseek-withholds-latest-ai-model-us-chipmakers-including-nvidia-sources-say-2026-02-25/)

- **RMB Stablecoin Ban + Crypto Capital Flight Shrinking:** PBOC banned RMB-linked stablecoins without government approval (Feb 6, 2026), covering domestic and offshore entities; simultaneously NPC pegged yuan stable at ~6.70/USD, reducing the capital-flight demand that historically drove Chinese retail into Bitcoin. [STNews](https://stnews.live/2026/03/05/stable-yuan-shrinking-flight-what-china-s-npc-means-for-crypto/amp/)

- **Digital Yuan Goes Interest-Bearing (Jan 1, 2026):** PBOC upgraded e-CNY from cash-equivalent to deposit money with interest-bearing wallets — a major architecture shift designed to compete with commercial bank deposits and accelerate CBDC adoption. [BeInCrypto](https://beincrypto.com/china-digital-yuan-interest-ban-crypto-2026/)

- **PLA Airspace Violation at Pratas Island:** In January 2026, a PLA WZ-7 "Soaring Dragon" drone became the first PLA aircraft to violate Taiwan's airspace in years, flying over Pratas Island beyond the range of local air defenses — PLA framed it as "normal training." [ISW](https://understandingwar.org/research/china-taiwan/china-taiwan-update-january-23-2026/)

- **Taiwan Arms Deal at Risk of Collapse:** Taiwan faces a March 15 deadline to sign three US arms packages; domestic political gridlock may cause it to miss the deadline for the first time ever — and WSJ reports CCP's recent military purges were directly inspired by Russia's corruption failures in Ukraine. [ISW](https://understandingwar.org/research/china-taiwan/china-taiwan-update-march-1-2026/)

- **15th Five-Year Plan Carries Xi's Personal Imprint:** 2026 opens the 15th FYP cycle (2026-2030), explicitly designed to consolidate Xi's legacy ahead of the 21st Party Congress in 2027 — all economic levers pointed at "high-quality development" and tech self-reliance, no pivot expected. [Asia Society](https://asiasociety.org/policy-institute/china-2026-what-watch)

## Edge Signal
The DeepSeek V4 selective hardware access policy is effectively creating a two-tier AI chip ecosystem — Chinese AI models optimized for Chinese silicon first — making Huawei's Ascend series the de facto domestic AI compute standard before Western observers have noticed the lock-in.

## Connects To
The stablecoin ban + stable yuan combo directly reduces Chinese retail's demand for offshore crypto as a capital escape valve, while the interest-bearing digital yuan captures that latent savings demand domestically — net bearish pressure on USDT OTC volumes from China, and a template for CBDC-vs-stablecoin competition globally.
