# China Researcher - 2026-03-08

## Top Findings

- **Xi's Military Purge Hits Top General**: Zhang Youxia, China's highest-ranking general and vice chairman of the Central Military Commission, placed under investigation - described as "the most sweeping [purge] in Chinese Communist Party history." This signals Xi is consolidating military control ahead of potential Taiwan action. [Source: NYT](https://www.nytimes.com/2026/02/03/opinion/china-military-purge-taiwan.html)

- **Xi Makes Currency Ambition Explicit**: In a rare Qiushi (CCP flagship journal) publication, Xi called for building a "powerful currency" to "attain reserve currency status." This isn't trial balloon territory - Qiushi is where the party formalizes priorities after internal consensus. The destination is now named. [Source: War on the Rocks](https://warontherocks.com/2026/02/xi-has-made-chinas-currency-ambition-explicit/)

- **Fourth Taiwan Strait Crisis Escalating**: "Strait Thunder–2025A" drills simulating seizure and blockade of Taiwan triggered joint G7 statement expressing "deep concern about China's provocative activities." These are the largest military exercises around Taiwan to date. [Source: Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fourth_Taiwan_Strait_Crisis)

- **Huawei + DeepSeek Breaking US Chip Reliance**: Beijing has urged tech giants to cease buying Nvidia chips tailored for China. Huawei unveiled Ascend chip series roadmap - first disclosure since 2019 blacklist. DeepSeek shifting to domestically produced chips. [Source: SCMP](https://www.scmp.com/tech/tech-war/article/3327025/home-grown-heroes-how-huawei-and-deepseek-are-helping-china-break-reliance-us-chips)

- **China's "Four Dragons" Chip Surge**: A wave of Chinese chipmakers raising billions and preparing IPOs. Huawei and Cambricon ramping production while four startups dubbed "Four Dragons" prepare listings. The next "DeepSeek moment" may be hardware, not models. [Source: WhatJobs](https://www.whatjobs.com/news/chinas-next-deepseek-moment-wont-be-a-model-it-will-be-hardware/)
